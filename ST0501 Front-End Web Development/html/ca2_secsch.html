My Secondary School Experience

My Secondary School Experience

School Life

My Secondary School was Jurong Secondary School, located in Taman Jurong. My secondary school journey has been a fruitful one as I got to take part in many activities and events such as the Overseas Immersion trip to Tianjin/Beijing and Student Leaders' Camp. Strong bonds were also forged betweeen my friends and I and we made unforgettable memories.

A Memorable Experience

My time with my secondary school class has also been a memorable one. We participated in many activities together and the most unforgettable one was the Overseas Service Learning Trip where we volunteered to do hard labour at a local school along the Mekong Delta such as painting the school gates and cementing the floor. We also prepared activities such as Chapteh and Uno for the children to enjoy and it was a great experience.

My CCA Journey

I joined Dragonboat in 2017 when I was Secondary 1 and I never regretted it since. It was a remarkable journey and I had a lot of takeaways such as not giving up even when facing the hardest obstacles. My teammates were very supportive of each other during trainings and we were really motivated to win our races. It was a fufilling experience I would never forget.

Temasek Regatta 2019

We climbed to the top of the Great Wall of China in Beijing. It was an accomplishment for the 5 of us as it was a tiring journey but we persevered through it nonetheless.
