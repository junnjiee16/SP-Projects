My Experience in SP

My Experience in SP

Life on Campus

It has been almost 2 months in Singapore Poly and the experience has been great. Some good things I found about campus is that there are 6 food courts so one will never get tired of the food. There is also a sports complex and a gym that is free for all students so there is no need for students to go out of school to workout. SP also has multiple libraries and studying spots all around the school, thus making campus a very convenient place for students.

CCA

SP has over 100 CCAs, from arts and cultural clubs to sports. Students can feel free to join multiple CCAs as there is no limit. I joined Canoe Sprint as I wanted to continue competitive rowing and the trainings have been tough but everyone is working hard towards to achieve their goals. If you are looking towards a vibrant campus life, you should definitely consider SP as one of your post-secondary/post-ITE choices!

School of Computing

My experience with School of Computing has been enjoyable. Stepping into the facility everyday learning what I like and intermingling with like-minded people has been a blast as there is not one moment where I was bored. Hilltop Library is also located at SOC so students can head over there after lessons to study or use the meeting cubicles to discuss group projects.
